Zitan

Zitan is a part of the newly masterplanned Greenfield District, a 12-hectare property bounded by EDSA, Shaw Boulevard, Sheridan and Reliance Streets.

Through the smart and connected blueprint of the Greenfield District, Zitan is part of a technoogically advanced urban center that measures up to, if not surpasses, the highest urban planning standards in the world.

Through Greenfield’s vision of world-class sustainable development that simultaneously works with nature and employs the latest technological advancements, Greenfield District shall stand as the highest standard-bearer of cosmopolitan living in the years to come.
