Reservation Agreement

I/We (the "Reservor") hereby submit a reservation for the lot/unit described above (the "Property") to EQUUS PROPERTY VENTURE, INC. (the "Seller") under the following terms and conditions:

1. This Reservation Agreement constitutes an offer to purchase the Property which shall be valid for a period of 30 days from date hereof. No other right, title or ownership is vested upon the Reservor by reason of the execution of this Reservation Agreement or payment of any amount due hereunder.

2. The Total Contract Price shall be valid only for the payment scheme that the Reservor has selected as indicated above and for the manner of payment as specified herein. Any change in the manner of payment and/or payment scheme shall be effective only upon the PRIOR written approval of the SELLER. The Reservation Fee shall beapplied and shall form part of the down payment (for installment sales) or the Total Contract Price (for cash/spot sales), as the case maybe, upon the execution of the Contract to Sell.

3. The Reservor represents, warrants and covenants that (i) this Reservation Agreement constitutes the legal, valid and binding obligation of the Reservor; (ii) all information provided by the Reservor in connection with this Reservation Agreement, is true, accurate and not misleading; (iii) the purchase of the Property is subject to the Project's Deed of Restrictions and Rules and Regulations by the Homeowners'/Locators' Association, as the case maybe, which shall be complied by the Reservor and annotated on the corresponding certificate of title to the Property as a voluntary lien thereon; (v) Reservor shall have no objections to the formation of a Homeowners'/Locators' Association by the SELLER, and agrees to become a member thereof, and pay association dues, assessments, and/or common area fee, when required by the SELLER; (vi) Reservor shall submit on or before the due date therefor all documentary and other requirements necessary for the execution of Contract to Sell to enable the Reservor to execute the Contract to Sell within 30 days from date hereof.

4. The Reservor acknowledges that the SELLER is undertaking or will be undertaking work to further develop or improve the Project or the surrounding areas, and that the development plans for the Project maybe changed by the SELLER. The Reservor confirms that the ongoing nature of such work at the time of this reservation shall not be a cause for the Reservor's cancellation of this reservation or provide of any basis for any reimbursement from the SELLER for any inconvenience or disturbance which may arise as a result for in connection with such work.

5. Check payments shall only be credited as payments made by the Reservor when such checks are accepted and honored by a local drawee bank engaged in business in Metro Manila. Brokers and real estate agents are not authorized to receive any cash payments. Cash payments in local and foreign currency, or checks in foreign currency shall be directly remitted to the office of the Cashier of the SELLER located at the Greenfield Corporate Center, No. 88 United Street, Greenfield District, Mandaluyong City. Any cash or check payment made to a party other than the above-enumerated shall be the sole and exclusive risk and responsibility of the Reservor.

6. This reservation shall be subject to the prior approval of the SELLER. However, in the event that the Reservor (i) cancels this reservation for any reason, (ii) fails to pay any amount when due or breaches any of the terms, conditions, representations or warranties contained in this reservation agreement, or requirements or policies of the SELLER, including the requirement the Reservor to submit to a full credit investigation, or (iii) fails to execute the Contract to Sell within 30 days from date hereof, and make the required payment thereunder, then the SELLER shall be entitled, without need of court declaration and upon issuance of prior written notice, to cancel this reservation agreement and forfeit in the SELLER's favor the reservation fee and all other amounts paid in connection with this Reservation Agreement.

7. The Reservor may assign all of its/her/his rights and obligations under this Reservation Agreement only with the prior written consent of the SELLER and subject to the payment of transfer fee of One Hundred Thousand Pesos (PhP 100,000.00) and reimbursement of the value-added and creditable withholding taxes paid by the SELLER. Should the SELLER so consent, the Reservor shall obtain the written consent of its/her/his assignee or transferee to the payment schemes selected by the Reservor and the manner of the payment of the Contract Price as specified herein.

8. Any notice required hereunder shall be sent to the address of the Reservor as indicated above either by personal delivery, courier or registered mail. Notices shall be deemed received (i) upon delivery, if sent by personal delivery, or (ii) within 5 days from posting in the Philippines, if sent by registered local mail or by courier; or (iii) within 14 days from posting outside the Philippines, if sent by registered mail, airmail or by courier.

9. The Reservor acknowledges that he/she/it has read and understood the checklist of required documents to be submitted to the SELLER in support of this Reservation on or before the required due dates
